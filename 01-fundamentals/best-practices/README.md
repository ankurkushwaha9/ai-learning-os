## üåü Golden Rules

### 1. Always Verify Important Information
AI can hallucinate. Double-check facts, especially for critical decisions.

### 2. Iterate and Refine
Your first prompt usually isn't your best. Improve through iteration.

### 3. Document What Works
Save successful prompts and patterns for reuse.

### 4. Understand Limitations
Know what AI can and can't do well.

### 5. Protect Sensitive Data
Be mindful of what information you share with AI tools.
