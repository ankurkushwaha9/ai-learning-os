# Do's and Don'ts

---

## ✅ DO

### Prompting
- ✅ Be specific about what you want
- ✅ Provide relevant context
- ✅ Use examples for complex formats
- ✅ Specify output format
- ✅ Iterate and refine prompts

### Verification
- ✅ Fact-check important information
- ✅ Test code before using in production
- ✅ Review generated content
- ✅ Cross-reference with reliable sources

### Organization
- ✅ Save successful prompts
- ✅ Document what works (and what doesn't)
- ✅ Build reusable templates
- ✅ Organize by use case

### Learning
- ✅ Experiment with different approaches
- ✅ Learn from failures
- ✅ Stay updated on new features
- ✅ Share knowledge with others

---

## ❌ DON'T

### Prompting
- ❌ Be vague or ambiguous
- ❌ Assume AI knows your context
- ❌ Give up after one try
- ❌ Use overly complex single prompts

### Trust
- ❌ Blindly trust AI outputs
- ❌ Use for critical decisions without verification
- ❌ Assume recent information is accurate
- ❌ Share highly sensitive data carelessly

### Workflow
- ❌ Forget to save good prompts
- ❌ Reinvent the wheel each time
- ❌ Ignore consistent errors/patterns
- ❌ Skip documentation
